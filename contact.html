Frequently Asked Questions

How do I schedule a property viewing?

You can schedule a property viewing by filling out the contact form above, calling our office directly, or sending us an email. Our team will get back to you within 24 hours to arrange a convenient time for your visit.

What documents do I need to purchase a property?

To purchase a property, you'll need valid identification (National ID, Passport, or Driver's License), proof of address, and proof of income or bank statements. Additional documentation may be required depending on the specific property and payment plan.

Do you offer financing options?

Yes, we offer various financing options and payment plans to make property acquisition more accessible. These include installment plans, mortgage partnerships with select banks, and developer financing options. Contact our sales team for details specific to your situation.

What are the costs associated with purchasing land?

Besides the property cost, additional expenses may include survey fees, legal fees, registration fees, development levy, and in some cases, infrastructure costs. We provide a transparent breakdown of all costs before any purchase commitment.
